About Us

Our downtown Saskatoon dental clinic is located in Midtown Plaza, and we are currently welcoming new patients of all ages. As general dentists we assess, provide treatment and instruction for the youngest of children in our designated separate children’s dental area, while adults, senior family members, and professionals are treated in our adjoining traditional clinic. We also welcome patient referrals of your family and friends, as our family and friends are the foundation of our business. We want your experience to be genuine with trust in our professionals and you are treated as we wish to be treated. We practice all general aspects of dentistry including check-ups, cleanings, fillings, extractions, crowns, bridges, implants, veneers, root canal therapy, whitening, aligners, guards and prosthetics. We work with an extensive network of healthcare practitioners, laboratories, physicians, and dental specialists to ensure you receive optimal oral care. We are members of the Canadian Dental Association and the College of Dental Surgeons of Saskatchewan.

Dr. Kathrina Robles

Dr. Robles is proud to be born and raised here in Saskatoon. In 2004, she completed a 4 year Bsc. in Microbiology & Immunology and in 2008, obtained her Dental Degree, both from the University of Saskatchewan. In 2008, after completing her D.M.D., Dr. Robles was accepted into the General Practice Residency Program at the Royal University Hospital, where she worked as a Dental Resident. There, Dr. Robles worked with a multidisciplinary medical/dental team to treat medically compromised patients, in addition to treating trauma patients. After completing her residency, Dr. Robles entered general practice. In 2014, Dr. Robles joined Viva Dental Studio as an associate, and later in 2016, took over as owner. Outside of work, Dr. Robles enjoys traveling, cooking, gardening and spending time with her husband and 2 sons. Dr. Robles feels privileged to be a part of the Viva Dental Studio team and looks forward to meeting you and your family!
